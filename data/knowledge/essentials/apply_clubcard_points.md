# Essentials — Apply Clubcard Points

**Systems used:** Carehub, Hansen

1. [Carehub] Open Hansen
2. [Hansen] Click on Apply Charges/Clubcard points
3. [Hansen] Select credit clubcard points
4. [Hansen] Enter desired clubcard point amount
5. [Hansen] Enter Reference
6. [Hansen] Select appropriate product from drop down
7. [Hansen] Click next
8. [Hansen] Confirm status message
9. [Hansen] Add brief notes
